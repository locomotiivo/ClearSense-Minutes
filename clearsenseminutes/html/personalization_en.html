Personalization

Personalization allows you to adjust the volume of each frequency band according to your auditory condtion. Since sensitivity to sound varies by frequency, you can adjust accordingly.

1 : Help, opens the help page you are currently viewing.

2 : Left ear, adjusts the output corresponding to the left ear.

3 : Right ear, adjusts the output corresponding to the right ear.

4 : 500Hz adjustment slider, adjusts the volume for 500Hz and lower frequencies.

5 : 1000Hz adjustment slider, adjusts the volume for 1000Hz and surrounding frequencies.

6 : 3000Hz adjustment slider, adjusts the volume for 3000Hz and higher frequencies.

7 : Listen, plays the sound corresponding to the selected frequency. Adjust until it sounds similar to other frequencies.

8 : Mirror to other ear, applies the same settings to the other ear.

9 : Reset, restores all settings to their previous values. The previous values are the ones from when you first entered the personalization page.

10 : Default, disables the personalization feature and sets all slider values to 0.
