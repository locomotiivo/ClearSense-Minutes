Main Screen

1 : File Manager. This is where you can check, share, and manage recorded files.

2 : Subscription Check. This is where you can check and manage your current subscription.

3 : Action Button. This button starts the operation of ClearSense Audio. Please use it after connecting your earphones/headset.

4 : Function Control Button. This button applies the noise reduction technology(Voice Mode) or allows you to hear the microphone sound as it is (Bypass Mode).

5 : Automatic Gain Control Button. When using Voice Mode, this button enables (AGC Mode) or disables (Normal Mode) AGC feature.

6 : Volume Mute Button. Touch to mute the volume.

7 : Volume Control Slider. You can adjust the volume. Please set it to an appropriate level.

8 : Recording Button. Touch to activate the recording feature. Recording only occurs when the app is in operation. The recorded data is managed internally on your phone, and mpWAV Inc. does not collect this data.

9 : Info. This is where you can check the terms of service, privacy policy, and licenses.

10 : Settings. This is where you can adjust various settings related to ClearSense Audio.

Settings

1 : Select the language. Currently, English and Korean are supported.

2 : Select the speaker output device.

3 : If you prefer natural background noise, you can leave some noise. Adjust the level of noise with the slider.

4 : You can set the equalizer. Voice presets and detailed settings are provided.

5 : Open the help section you are currently viewing.

6 : Send an email to mpWAV Inc.

7 : Quick guide of how to use ClearSense Audio
